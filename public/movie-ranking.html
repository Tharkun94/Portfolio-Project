Top Movies of All Time According to Austin

My Favorite 3 Movies

Lord of the Rings

Honestly my favorite movies ever. Beautful story with high stakes and rich emotional moments

Star Wars

When i am not geeking out about Lord of The Rings, I am likely geeking out about Star Wars. The universe itself is a fantastic place to get lost in, but not all of the movies are home runs. However that is neither here nor there

Godzilla

Truly a guilty pleasure. A long series about a big, nuclear lizard who is virtually indusctructable. What's not to love?
